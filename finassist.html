Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
By default your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It stays on the device unless you export or opt into Pro backups.
Can I backup or transfer data?
You can export CSV/PDF files or download a JSON backup. Pro will add encrypted cloud backups and restores.
Is the app free?
FinAssist core is free. FinAssist Pro will be a paid tier with advanced capabilities. Free users keep essential functionality.
Will there be an Android/iOS app?
FinAssist is a PWA-ready web app — installable on mobile home screens. We may release native wrappers later for Pro users.
How secure is Pro backup?
Pro backups will be encrypted client-side. Only you hold the decryption key; backups are optional and reversible.
